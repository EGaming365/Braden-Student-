Mockups

‎

The Mockups project features detailed wireframes designed to map out the user experience and interface of a website. Created with a focus on functionality and usability, these wireframes outline the layout of key elements such as navigation menus, content sections, and interactive features. Each mockup is structured to prioritize user flow, ensuring intuitive access to information. The design process involved iterative revisions based on feedback, showcasing a commitment to improving user interaction. By employing design principles such as hierarchy and balance, the wireframes effectively communicate the envisioned aesthetic and operational aspects of the final website, serving as a crucial blueprint for development.

‎

The Mockups project involved creating wireframes for a workout website, focusing on user engagement and functionality. To start, I conducted research to understand the needs and preferences of fitness enthusiasts, which guided the overall design direction. I utilized tools like Figma to sketch out the initial layouts, emphasizing a clean and intuitive user interface. Each wireframe illustrates key components such as the homepage, workout categories, and user profiles, ensuring easy navigation. I incorporated elements like progress tracking, video tutorials, and community features, prioritizing user experience. Iterative feedback sessions allowed for refinement of the designs, ensuring they effectively conveyed the intended functionality and aesthetic of the final product. This thorough approach resulted in wireframes that serve as a solid foundation for the website's development.
